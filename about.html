The Founder

White Sage was founded by Catherin van Heerden, a holistic practitioner with over 15 years of experience in kinesiology and energy healing.

Her approach blends scientific understanding of the body with intuitive awareness of emotional and energetic patterns. Each session is tailored uniquely — no two journeys are the same.

Clients often describe their sessions as grounding, clarifying, and deeply transformative.

What Is Kinesiology?

Kinesiology is a holistic therapy that uses gentle muscle response testing to identify stress patterns and energetic imbalances within the body.

By accessing the body's natural feedback system, kinesiology helps uncover subconscious stress, emotional blockages, and physical tension.

The goal is not just symptom relief — but restoring harmony between mind, body and spirit.
